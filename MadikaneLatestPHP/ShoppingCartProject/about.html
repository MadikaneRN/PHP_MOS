ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

MOS… Is an organization that is focused on creating a commercially sustainable and community driven initiative.

MOS… Provides an opportunity of empowerment for individuals / NPO’s and entities across South Africa and the continent.

MOS… Is a platform for stimulating economic and social change. As the saying goes: “If you give a man a fish, he eats for a day. Teach a man to fish, feed him for a lifetime.”

MOS… Manufactures quality products at an affordable rate, for individuals to sell directly or distribute them at a profit, creating a sustainable revenue.

MOS… Is the proverbial fishing rod that enables individuals to self-empower.

EMPOWERMENT THROUGH PLEASE CLOTHING PRODUCTS

MOS… Promotes doing business with good intentions.

MOS… Represents ideas of hope, freedom and change.

MOS… Carries a message of empowerment and personal responsibility, for ourselves, our communities and our world.

MOS… Endeavors to create more products that offers ways of empowerment.

MOS… Believes there is greatness in everyone.

MOS… Believes in self-sustainability.
